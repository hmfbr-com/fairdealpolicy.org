Templates & standards layer

FDP is anchored in the Spine (10 Articles). Templates are how people use FDP in real contexts. This page hosts the stable structure that keeps templates coherent while allowing local variation.

The Template Skeleton is a canonical grammar (structure-first). Template families and concrete templates will evolve on top of it.
