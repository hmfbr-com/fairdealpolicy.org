Fairness-Intelligence

Use this prompt before you commit. It does not judge morality or legality — it makes outset conditions visible. For the conceptual spine, see Pre-Commit Foundations.

Copy-paste prompt

Paste into any LLM. Provide your situation under “INPUT”. You’ll receive: (1) Outset Vector, (2) OFP, (3) Top 3 leverage edits, (4) plain-language “Before you commit”.

Worked examples (v0)

Example A — Platform ToS + “neutrality” claim
Input: long ToS, opaque ranking, opt-out data sharing, unilateral changes, high lock-in.
Ask the model to propose 3 outset edits that reduce power asymmetry and increase clarity.
Example B — Freelancer deal via marketplace
Input: scope ambiguity, revision creep, reputational exit friction, disputes skewed.
Ask for rewritten “Before you commit” language + scope caps.
Example C — Procurement brief before first bid
Input: incumbent-biased framing, risk dumped on suppliers, compressed timeline.
Ask for leverage edits: pre-market dialogue, proportional risk, timeline sanity.
